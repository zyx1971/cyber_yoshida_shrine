About

The Cyber Yoshida Shrine was founded in 1997 as a virtual shrine on the web.
Yoshida-no-Kami is a deity believed to reside in the seas of Wakayama, worshipped by those seeking protection from project failures and the safe go-live of IT systems.
The shrine is said to carry the sacred energy of Mount Koya and bestow divine blessings relevant even in today’s digital society.
